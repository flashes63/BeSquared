Make every row and every column a word.

  • Fill in the squares with the letters given to make a word.
  • Press the 'check' button to check if the letters are in the right place.

  • A light blue box means the letter is in the right place.
  • Press the 'hint' button to check out the meanings of the words in each column and row.
  • Press the 'restart' button to restart the puzzle.
